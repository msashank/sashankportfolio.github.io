A collection of mechanical engineering projects spanning topology optimization, thermal systems, reverse engineering, and welding metallurgy.

01
nTopology SolidWorks DLP 3D Print

Lightweighting a Part Using nTopology

Designed and optimized a centrifuge-compatible holder for DLP 3D printed parts to remove excess unpolymerized resin. The holder features a slanted perforated surface and conical collection section, fabricated in PLA to withstand 300 rpm. Structural optimization via nTopology delivered dramatic weight savings without sacrificing mechanical integrity.

Key Result 71.5% mass reduction · 74.7% volume reduction vs original design
02
Thermal Systems Ray Tracing Ashby Charts

Solar Water Heater

Engineered an electronics-free solar water heating system capable of boiling 1 liter in under an hour — designed for backpackers. Split into a solar concentrator (optics-based, ray-traced) and a solar receiver (insulated absorber, heat-transfer optimized). Material selection guided by Ashby charts; cosmetic choices enhanced radiative absorption.

Key Result 1L boiled in 44 min · Zero fasteners · Fully electronics-free
03
Reverse Engineering DFA Analysis CAD Modeling

Reverse Engineering — Metronome

Disassembled and fully 3D modeled the Ueteto SLM-350 metronome, analyzing its escapement mechanism representing 200+ years of iterative refinement. Performed comprehensive DFA analysis, material audit, and hardware standardization study — identifying opportunities to reduce screw variants from 6 types (including custom thread pitches) down to just 2 standardized types.

Key Result 40% theoretical min part count · 57.5% assembly efficiency · Cost-optimized materials
04
FSW Metallurgy AA6082-T6

Friction Stir Welding — Hybrid Tool Pins

Compared square vs triangular tool pins in Friction Stir Welding of AA6082-T6 aluminium alloy across rotational speeds of 900, 1100, and 1400 rpm. Post-weld specimens were sectioned into dog-bone shapes for UTM tensile testing, and microstructure analyzed across Nugget Zone, HAZ, and TMAZ regions.

Key Result Triangular pins → superior strength & finer grain · Lower RPM → better weld quality
